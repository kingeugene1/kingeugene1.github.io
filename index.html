About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Tsukuba, specializing in Structural Engineering specifically the maintenance of bridge using drive-by bridge monitoring technologies. I hold a Master of Science and a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering. My research focuses on Structural Health Monitoring, Vibration Signal Processing, and Vehicle-Bridge Interaction. With a strong foundation in mathematical and problem-solving skills, I am also passionate about web development, with skills in HTML, CSS, and data analysis using Python. I am currently seeking a position as a Structural Engineer or a Junior Web Developer where I can apply my diverse skillset.
